French Fries

If you want to make crispy and delicious french fries at home, then you're going to love our recipe!

List of ingredients:

  • 2 fresh russet potatoes
  • 2 cups of refined oil
  • Salt as required
  • Black pepper for garnish

Step by step instructions:

  1. Chop the potatoes in the correct shape and size
  2. Soak the cut potatoes in ice-cold water for 10-15 minutes
  3. Deep fry the potatoes in a pan with the oil
  4. Cook until they reach a golden brown color
  5. Sprinkle salt & pepper and serve hot
